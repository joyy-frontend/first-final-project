Our Services

Full Groom

Includes a full-style haircut, a luxury bath with shampoo and conditioner, blow drying, full body brushing and styling, nail trimming and grinding, gentle ear cleansing, sanitary trim and anal gland expression. We finish with a stylish bandana and a spritz of revitalizing spray.

Mini Groom

Includes a luxury bath with shampoo and conditioner, blow drying, full body brushing and styling, nail trimming and grinding, gentle ear cleansing, sanitary trim, as well as a trim up of the face (eyes and visor), paws, tail and ears. We finish with a stylish bandana and a spritz of revitalizing spray.

Luxury Bath

Includes a luxurious bath experience with shampoo and conditioner, blow drying, nail trimming and grinding, gentle ear cleansing, full body brushing & extra love. We finish with a stylish bandana and spritz of revitalizing spray.

Classic Bath

Includes a standard bath with shampoo and conditioner, blow drying, gentle ear cleansing, and full body brushing.
